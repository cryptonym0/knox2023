ABOUT KNOX MOUNTAIN DOWNHILL

The Knox Mountain Downhill Race is an annual event co-founded by the Kamloops Longboard Club and Kelowna Longboard Alliance. Our goal is to foster the sport of downhill skateboarding in interior British Columbia, Canada. This event attracts a diverse group of athletes from around the globe, with over 60 participants in the last year. This year KMDH will take place May 31 - June 1 2025!


Spectators are welcome and encouraged to come out and support the riders!

  • Downhill Skateboarders and Street Lugers of all ages welcome
  • Official Qualifier for Team Canada
  • North American Downhill League Race Format
  • Top 4 fastest Skateboard athletes seeded directly into Semi-Finals
  • Top 2 fastest Luge athletes seeded directly into Semi-Finals
  • Cash Podium
  • Spectators Welcome

KMDH is proud to partner with Kelowna's #1 Hit Music Station.
